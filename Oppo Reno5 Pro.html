Oppo Reno5 Pro 5G: Know everything about it.

INTRODUCTION

The Chinese smartphone manufacturing company Oppo has now come up with a new 5G smartphone, Oppo Reno 5 Pro which can be pre ordered now in its official website through Flipkart. Here is almost everything you all need to know.

COLOURS

Oppo Reno 5 Pro is available in two colours: STARRY BLACK & ASTRAL BLUE

CAMERA

The camera is the most important and interesting feature of Oppo Reno 5 Pro. It comes with 4 cameras containing 64MP main camera, 8MP ultrawide camera, 2MP macro camera and 2MP mono camera. The most important and branded feature present in the camera is the AI highlight video (Ultra Night Video + Live HDR) and dual view video which captures with the front and rear camera simultaneously. Oppo Reno 5 Pro is also fitted with 32 megapixels punch hole selfie camera.

Screen & Display

Oppo Reno 5 Pro comes with 6.5 inches Super AMOLED curved HD quality display with 20:9 aspect ratio. Oppo Reno 5 Pro has 90Hz refresh rate with availability of 60Hz refresh rate. Oppo Reno 5 Pro has 3D borderless sense screen which gives it a slim and a beautiful look. Its streamline design makes it easy to hold. Oppo Reno 5 Pro has ultra slim body with thickness of about 7.6mm and weight of about 173g.

Processor

Oppo Reno 5 Pro has MediaTek Dimensity 1000+ Processor. Reno5 Pro comes equipped with MediaTek's 7nm-class Dimensity 1000+, a 5G chip that delivers superior performance and top-notch graphics powered by Arm Cortex-A77 and Mali-G77 cores. With support for Wi-Fi 6, you will gain stronger connection stability and faster speeds.

Charging

Oppo Reno 5 Pro comes with 65W SuperVOOC 2.0 charger with USB C cable in its box. Oppo Reno 5 Pro has 4380 MH battery and it gets fully charged in 30 mins. Also, a 5-minute charging gives the smartphone 4 hours of video playback.

OS

Oppo Reno 5 Pro has built in ColorOS 11.1 based on latest Android 11.

Specs & Price

Oppo Reno 5 Pro is now available in only 6GB RAM + 128GB ROM storage.

The smartphone is priced Rs.35,990 and available for pre-order.
